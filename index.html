Bharathi Raja Chakravarthi

PhD Student

I am Ph.D. student at Insight Centre for Data Analytics, National University of Ireland, Galway . I am working with Dr. John McCrae in Unit of Linguistic Data and Dr. Mihael Arčan, Unit of Natural Language Processing. My research area is Natural Language Processing and Machine Translation. Currently, my research focus is Machine Translation for under-resourced languages. I did my Masters in Computational Science from Indian Institute of Information Technology & Management-Kerala and B.Tech in Agricultural Information Technology from Tamil Nadu Agricultural University, Coimbatore.
